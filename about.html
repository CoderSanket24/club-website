About Us

Vishwanoids is an engineering college robotics club dedicated to innovation and learning. Our mission is to provide students with hands-on experience in robotics, automation, and AI technologies. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications by engaging students in practical projects, competitions, and research initiatives.

Founded in [Year], Vishwanoids has grown into a dynamic community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for robotics. We actively participate in various national and international competitions such as Robocon, Smart India Hackathon, and Tech Fests.

Our team consists of talented engineers, designers, and programmers who work together to develop cutting-edge robotic solutions. We also conduct workshops, seminars, and training sessions to equip members with the latest industry trends and technologies.
